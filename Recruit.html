Our Recruitment Procedure

Information Desk

Octopus Recruitment Pvt. Ltd. offers its service for its clients from its main information Service Desk from where any relevant information about recruitment service can be obtained. Our staffs are highly qualified so they will provide quality service to all the clients and the visitors.

Advertisement in Nepali Daily Newspaper

After receiving the all set of documents from party, we will apply these all documents to Department of Labor (DOL) at Nepal for authorization. The DOL will grant the approval on being satisfied about the credibility of the demand letter and attesting that terms and conditions offered are conformity with the government rules. The persmission allows the recruitment company to advertise the demand in Nepal's national daily newspaper. Then we are allowed to start the recruitment process as well as collect the passports and the candidates as per demand letter.

Selection of Candidate

From that advertisement when candidate came then we will fill up their data with full information about their education, qualification, skills for the post. Octopus Recruitment Pvt. Ltd. will screen the short-listed candidates by pre-interview. After this selection the final interview will be conducted by the employer or his representative or by Octopus Recruitment Pvt. Ltd. itself taking his practical, written and oral test. From this selection the employer are confident that the candidate whom they have selected are needed for their human resources.

Medical Check-up

The selected applicants will be sent to an authorized hospitals or clinic for their full medical check-up to ensure that they are physically and mentally fit before joining the company.

Visa Processing

All the medically fit candidates passports will be sent to related company for the visa applying.

Orientation

After receiving the visas, the candidate must join orientation class to aware about labor laws, immigration policy, country's law, country's tradition and to maintaining understand and cordial relation between employers and employees, which direct them to maintain good working relationship and motivates them to do their duties and responsibilities in perfessional manner.

Final Approval from DOL

We will submit all the set of documents, Medical fit report, and Orientation Certificate with visa copy for the last approval to the DOL.

Departure

After finishing all the above procedure we make a ticket for the candidate to the nearest airport of the working country. Before the flight of candidates, we will send the flight details for arrangement of clearance to the candidates in the Airport so that they are received on time.
